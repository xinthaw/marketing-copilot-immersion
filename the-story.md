# The Story

Meet **Contoso Marketing** — a fast-moving B2B team launching campaigns across email, social, web, and events. They have:

- A new product launch every quarter
- Tight content review cycles with Legal and Brand
- A small team that wears many hats
- Mountains of campaign data nobody has time to read

This immersion follows Maya, a Campaign Manager at Contoso, as she goes from *"I use Copilot for email"* to *"I built an agent that writes our launch briefs."*

Each scenario is something Maya actually does in her week. You'll do the same activity, with the same materials, in your own Copilot.

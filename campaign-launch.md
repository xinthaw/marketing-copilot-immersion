## What makes this different

This is not a Copilot feature demo. Every scenario is a real Marketing situation Contoso faces, and every activity ends with a usable output or a live tool your team can use tomorrow morning. Five supplementary scenarios are available if time permits.

## Three core activities

### Core 1 · Campaign Launch Coach
Research the next 24-hour launch window, draft the announcement email, calculate channel mix, and pull a stakeholder briefing — all in one Copilot Chat session.

### Core 2 · Content Pitch Coach
Microsoft's pre-built Pitch Coach agent runs on a prompt curated by Marketing leadership. Pick any blog or campaign brief from a curated list of 3, paste it in, answer two follow-up questions, and walk away with a structured pitch that actually works.

### Core 3 · Build a Brand Voice Agent
Use the M365 Copilot Agent Builder to build a self-service Brand Voice assistant. Feed it your tone-of-voice guide and three approved campaign emails — your team gets on-brand copy in seconds.

## Try it yourself

{: .note }
> **Prompt to try in Copilot Chat:**
>
> *"Act as a Campaign Launch Coach. We're launching [PRODUCT] on [DATE] to [AUDIENCE]. Draft a 24-hour launch checklist covering email, LinkedIn, web banner, and internal comms. For each channel, suggest the headline, the call-to-action, and the time of day to publish. Reference Contoso's brand voice: confident, plainspoken, no jargon."*

### What to look for

- Does the checklist cover all four channels?
- Are the headlines on-brand (no jargon, confident tone)?
- Did Copilot suggest a publishing schedule, not just content?

### Stretch goal

Ask Copilot to **turn the checklist into a table** you can paste into Excel, with columns for *Channel · Asset · Owner · Publish time · Status*.

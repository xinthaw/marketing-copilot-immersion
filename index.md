## What this is

A practical, scenario-driven series that takes marketers from **using Copilot in chat** to **building their own AI agents** for repeatable marketing work — campaign launches, content pitches, brand voice review, and analytics recaps.

## Who it's for

The Marketing team — campaign managers, content writers, brand leads, and marketing ops. No coding required.

## How it's organized

- **Core sessions** — the main learning path. Do these in order.
- **Supplementary activities** — bonus scenarios for extra practice.
- **Tools** — Copilot Chat, Copilot in Word/PowerPoint/Excel, and Copilot Studio (Agent Builder).

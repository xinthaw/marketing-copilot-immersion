# Core 2 · Content Pitch Coach

[COPILOT CHAT]{: .label .label-blue } [WORD]{: .label .label-purple } [75 MINUTES]{: .label .label-green }

Take any rough campaign idea and turn it into a structured pitch your CMO can sign off on — with Copilot as the coach.

## What you'll do

1. Pick one of three sample campaign briefs (B2B SaaS launch, retail seasonal push, event activation)
2. Open the brief in Word and run **Copilot · Rewrite as a pitch**
3. Answer two follow-up questions Copilot asks
4. Refine until you have a one-page pitch with: *Audience · Insight · Idea · Channels · Measure of success*

## The prompt

{: .note }
> *"You are a Content Pitch Coach. Read the brief I've pasted. Ask me up to two clarifying questions, then output a one-page pitch with these sections: Audience, Insight, Big Idea, Channels, Measure of Success. Keep it under 300 words."*

## What good looks like

- The pitch fits on one page
- The "Insight" is specific to the audience, not generic
- "Measure of Success" is a number, not a vibe

## Stretch goal

Save the prompt as a **reusable Copilot prompt** so the rest of your team can run it without retyping.

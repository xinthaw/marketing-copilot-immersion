# Get started

## Before you begin

1. Sign in to Microsoft 365 with your work account
2. Open Copilot Chat at [m365.cloud.microsoft/chat](https://m365.cloud.microsoft/chat)
3. Download the [sample files]({{ site.baseurl }}/sample-files) for your first session
4. Block 60–90 minutes on your calendar — these sessions are hands-on

## Recommended path

1. **Core 1** — [Campaign Launch Coach]({{ site.baseurl }}/campaign-launch)
2. **Core 2** — [Content Pitch Coach]({{ site.baseurl }}/content-pitch-coach)
3. **Core 3** — [Build a Brand Voice Agent]({{ site.baseurl }}/brand-voice-agent)
4. **Supplementary** — [Analytics Recap]({{ site.baseurl }}/analytics-recap) (when you have campaign data ready)

## Tips

{: .warning }
> Don't paste confidential data into public AI tools — use **work** Copilot only.

- Treat the prompts as a starting point. Tweak them with your team's voice and context.
- If a result looks off, refine the prompt rather than starting over. Iterate.
